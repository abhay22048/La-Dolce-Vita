Our Story

Established in 1989, La Dolce Vita has been a beloved destination for authentic Italian cuisine in Milton, Brisbane. Nestled under Australia’s only Eiffel Tower, our restaurant captures the charm of Italy with rich flavors, warm hospitality, and a passion for fine food. Whether you're here for a morning espresso, a long lunch, or a romantic dinner, we invite you to experience a slice of Italy in Brisbane.
